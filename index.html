About me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Sonya Edwards and I am a Senior at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville pursuing a degree in Business Administration majoring in Economics with a collateral in Business Analytics and Statistics and minoring in Child and Family Studies. This portfolio details my growth during my time in undergraduate by highlighting some of my most important experiences. UTK has cultivated my growth and learning in more ways than I ever could have imagined. Throughout my time here, I have developed academic skills, such as learning to think analytically, and I have also developed interpersonal skills, such as a more clear sense of identity and personal values. My career aspirations involve developing community ties in a way that will allow me to more effectively serve alongside others. My time in AmeriCorps inspired me to want to work alongside disadvantaged communities, particularly in a family services or community economic development role. As I have seen how pertinent education is to a sustainable, healthy future, I would like a career advocating for quality education and family service. Some things I enjoy outside of my professional aspirations are spending time with others, being outside in nature, yoga, and reading. These aspects of my life are important for maintaining a balanced sense of health and happiness. As a people-oriented person, feeling a sense of community has always been incredibly important to me. Nature has always been a great way for me to relax. Reading has opened up my imagination in more ways than I could have imagined. Yoga has taught me how to take a step back and focus inwards.
